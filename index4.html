SECURITY NOTICE

Please read the following security information carefully before proceeding with the online banking website


  • Ensure that you are accessing the afficial and secure banking website or any online transfer.
  • Do not share your username, password, TAC/OTP, or any confidential information.
  • Verify the recipient's bank account details, E-wallet and transfer amount carefully
  • Avoid using public or unsecured devices for online transfer
  • Always log out after completing your transaction.
  • Report any suspicious or unauthorized activities immediately
